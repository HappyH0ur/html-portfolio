The Best Movies According to Kristen

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Pretty Woman

This is my favourite film with Julia Roberts. I love the story and her character.

My Cousin Vinny

This movie is hilarity at its finest. Joe Pesci and Marisa Tomei are a dynamo duo who make every bit of this film, serious or not, relatable and funny.

Lake Placid

What's funny about a giant crocodile trying to eat people in a lake? Witty repertoire from the entire cast, including Betty White!
